WELCOME TO ILLUSTREMOR!

An earthquake is the shaking of the surface of the Earth which can cause a sudden release of energy in the lithosphere (Earth's outermost layer), creating seismic waves. Earthquakes can vary in size and range, they go from being so weak they may not be felt, to so strong and violent they can shake entire cities and cause destruction. We can measure the scale and severity of an earthquake by finding it's magnitude. ILLUSTREMOR allows us to show and report recorded earthquakes and their relevant information like magnitude across the United States.
